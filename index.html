Full bio

Vítor Holtz

My name is Vítor Holtz, brazilian, graduated from UNIVALI (Universidade do Vale do Itajaí) in Marketing and Advertising. I’ve been working with design since 2011. Design for me isn’t just about visuals, it’s about a whole project where people analyze and take in consideration everything that hover around the customer problem. My approach is based on a teamwork basis, where I’m always trying to bring both my customers and my colleagues to the core of the project. I work with Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe XD and After effects. I have good technical knowledge in HTML, CSS and a little JavaScript.
